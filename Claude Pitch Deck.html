Remember Me Not?

Bridging the Gap in Cognitive Care.

Productization & MVP Pitch

The Solution

For the Patient

A deeply personalized cognitive care app that replaces abstract clinical tasks with familiar stimuli.

  • Memory-matching with family photos.
  • Therapeutic, familiar music playlists.
  • An AI-driven Spaced-Repetition algorithm that adapts difficulty daily.

For the Guardian

A dedicated dashboard to monitor progress, alleviate burnout, and keep the family connected.

  • Real-time analytics on patient accuracy.
  • Remote content management for photos/audio.
  • AI alerts when specific memories begin to fade.
