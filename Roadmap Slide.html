ROADMAP

1
STARTUP WEEKEND
Startup Weekend enabled us to build a strong team, gain expert feedback, and validate our idea through real user insights and continuous iteration.
2
PROOF OF EFFECTIVENESS
Effectiveness is ensured through a research-driven approach, incorporating input from IT experts and early caregiver validation, alongside planned usability testing and measurable outcomes in real-world care settings.
3
POC
A Proof of Concept (PoC) funding application from the University of Oulu: prototype of a human-centred, AI-supported serious game application to support cognitive engagement and caregiver interaction in dementia care.
4
RESEARCH TO BUSINESS
Apply for Research to Business funding. The project bridges academic research and real-world application by transforming validated insights into a scalable, commercially viable healthcare solution, and prepares the commercialization of research results into new business opportunities.
5
SPIN OFF COMPANY
Following validation and pilot testing, we aim to establish a spin-off company to drive commercialization and scale the solution within the healthcare sector.
6
SCALE TO GLOBAL
After establishing traction in Finland, we aim to scale internationally by adapting the solution to different healthcare systems and forming global partnerships.
