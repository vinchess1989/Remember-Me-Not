Remember Me Not?

Date: 17.04.2026
Designers: Mary, Joel, Arnold, Eugenia, Vineeth

1 Problem

  • Patients with dementia & their family caretakers.
  • Need for accessible brain training, daily exercise, and improved communication.
  • Existing Alternatives: Muistipuisto, offline activities, general online platforms.

2 Solution

  • Authorized monitoring and editing by families.
  • Deeply personalized interactive elements (photos, music).
  • Rebuilding connections via familiar topics and daily schedules.

3 Key Metrics

  • Daily active usage and streak maintenance.
  • Identification accuracy and speed metrics.
  • Time delayed before transitioning to institutional care.

4 Unique Value Proposition

A deeply personalized cognitive care ecosystem linked directly to the user's specific background and family network, replacing generic, abstract brain games.

5 Unfair Advantage

  • Integrated daily ecosystem paired with a future Phase 4 hardware roadmap (Smart Olfactory diffusers and NFC Reminiscence boxes).

6 Channels

  • Direct pitching and B2G sales to municipal healthcare providers.
  • App Stores (iOS/Android) direct-to-consumer.

7 Customer Segments

  • Target Customers: Patient caretakers, Government (B2G), Early-stage patients.
  • Early Adopters: Families struggling with daily communication, and municipalities looking to save government money and return caretaker labor to the working market.

8 Cost Structure

  • App development and continuous maintenance.
  • HIPAA/GDPR compliant secure cloud hosting.
  • Hardware R&D (IoT integration).

9 Revenue Streams

  • B2G licensing directly to wellbeing services counties.
  • B2C premium subscriptions for families.
  • Future hardware peripheral sales.
